Can’t Get Enough: Jac Caglianone’s Wholesome Reaction to Call-Up

Jac Caglianone, the tenth-ranked prospect in baseball, is set to make his debut for the Kansas City Royals.

His reaction to being called up was incredible.

“I need to call my dad,” Caglianone said.

The No. 6 overall pick in the 2024 MLB Draft is in position to elevate Kansas City’s outfield. In his 79-game stint in the minors, he batted .322 with 27 home runs.

The Royals currently sit in fourth place in the AL Central despite having a respectable 31-29 record. Perhaps Caglianone can help them chase down a playoff spot.
