Can’t Get Enough: Dustin Jacoby Scores Brutal Knockout Win

UFC light heavyweight contender Dustin Jacoby (21-9-1) disposed of Bruno Lopes in less than two minutes of their fight on Saturday at UFC on ESPN 68.

The 37-year-old’s win marked his second in a row and ninth in the UFC. “The Hanyak” now has 14 career wins by knockout.

It was the only knockout on Saturday’s card, where six of the nine fights went to a decision. Surprisingly, though, Jacoby was not awarded a post-fight bonus for his standout performance.
