Can’t Get Enough: Five NBA Teams to Receive Classic Jersey for 2025-26 Season

According to a report from basketbaljerseyarchive.com, five teams will receive a Classic Edition Jersey for the 2025-26 NBA season, and fans are fired up.

The Cavaliers, Mavericks, Grizzlies, Timberwolves and 76ers will sport throwback uniforms featuring the Nike Futura logo. It will be the second consecutive year that the Grizzlies receive a new Classic Edition Uniform.

Though the official designs have not been released, fans have begun to make predictions.
