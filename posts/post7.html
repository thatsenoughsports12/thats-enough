Can’t Get Enough: O’Malley, Peña to Wear Custom Shorts at UFC 316

Former bantamweight king Sean O’Malley will wear pink shorts in his rematch against champion Merab Dvalishvili at UFC 316 on Saturday.

O’Malley wore pink in his last victory, which came against Marlon Vera at UFC 299.

Two-time women’s bantamweight champion Julianna Peña will also be sporting custom threads in her co-main event bout against Kayla Harrison. Peña’s kit reflects her Venezuelan, Mexican, American, and Aztec roots.
