That’s Enough: Orlando Magic Unveil Hideous New Uniforms

The Orlando Magic attempted to return to their 90s roots as they released three new uniforms to be worn during the 2025-26 NBA season. Unfortunately, they failed miserably.

introducing a new generation of Magic basketball pic.twitter.com/V8DWuZdpSG

Magic fans have been calling for a rebrand for quite some time, but I’m not sure this is what they envisioned.

I have no issue with the pinstripes, as they pay homage to the classic uniforms worn in the 90s. However, it feels like they couldn’t decide if they wanted to fully commit to their old uniforms. The font of the letters and numbers is basic, modern, and does not resemble the threads worn 30 years ago.

The home and away uniforms might grow on me, but the black jersey looks like it was designed by a middle schooler. There’s no reason for the blue up top. It’s just too much.

As expected, Magic fans aren’t thrilled with the changes.

Moral of the story: if you’re going to do a throwback, do a throwback. Don’t modernize it.
